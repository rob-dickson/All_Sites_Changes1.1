Welcome to Brand Fulfillment

Brand fulfillment is when a brand directly delivers orders placed on a retailer's website, instead of the retailer's supply chain handling the order. This can give brands more control over their relationships with retailers, including sales data, marketing, and pricing. It can also help brands increase their margins by removing the middleman.

There are several types of brand fulfillment, including marketplace, drop ship, and digital concessions. Drop shipping is a fulfillment method where a third-party supplier ships products directly to the consumer, and the seller doesn't usually have possession of the product.
