About Us

BK Machine was founded in 1980 by Ben Kone, specializing initially in precision manufacturing for the medical sector. The company swiftly garnered a reputation for its commitment to superior quality and customer satisfaction. In 1998, Jeff Kone assumed leadership, steering BK Machine towards expansion both in scale and product diversity. Recognizing untapped potential in the firearm accessory market, BK Machine diversified its offerings, leading to the establishment of Tactical Precision MFG in 2012. Since its inception, Tactical Precision MFG has catered to a diverse clientele with its range of firearm accessories. Today, as a family-owned enterprise, BK Machine and Tactical Precision MFG continue their legacy of delivering exceptional products and services directly to consumers and firearm suppliers alike through their dedicated online platform. Their unwavering commitment remains steadfast: to provide superior craftsmanship, competitive pricing, and personalized customer care.
